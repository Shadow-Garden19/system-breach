HOW IT WORKS
Goal
Reveal safe cards to grow your Current Win. Avoid Virus cards.
Bet & Current Win
  • Bet: the amount you choose before starting.
  • Current Win: your live pot for this round. Each safe card multiplies it.
  • You can Cash Out anytime after at least one safe reveal to collect the Current Win.
Explosions (Viruses)
More explosions means higher risk. Hitting a Virus ends the round and you lose the bet for that round.
Auto Cashout
Enable Auto Cashout and set a target multiplier. When your total multiplier reaches the target, the game cashes out automatically.
Fairness & transparency
  • Each safe card displays its multiplier. Your Current Win updates accordingly.
  • Your Balance only changes when you start a round (bet is taken) and when you cash out (winnings are added).
